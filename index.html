About

Theory meets experiment

Founded in 2024 at UNJBG, Tacna. We close the loop from DFT prediction to laboratory synthesis and characterization. Funded by Canon Minero.

Develop nanomaterials for energy conversion and storage.

Characterize compounds with advanced spectroscopic techniques.

Run HPC simulations for electronic and transport properties.

Optimize thermoelectric ZT and ionic conductivity computationally.
