About the group

Theory meets experiment

GEM was founded in 2024 at Universidad Nacional Jorge Basadre Grohmann. Our research loop closes from DFT prediction to laboratory synthesis, characterization, and back — theory-guided design for real energy applications. Funded by Canon Minero through UNJBG's competitive research program.

Key objectives

Develop new nanomaterials with technological potential for energy conversion and storage.

Synthesize and characterize compounds using advanced spectroscopic and microscopic techniques.

Implement HPC simulations to study structural, electronic, and transport properties from first principles.

Optimize thermoelectric ZT and ionic conductivity in solid electrolytes via computational screening.
